About Makerere University Library

Established in 1922, Makerere University Library is the oldest and largest university library in Uganda. We are committed to providing a comprehensive and supportive learning environment for our students, faculty, and researchers.

Our Mission

To provide access to high-quality information resources and services that support the academic, research, and personal development needs of the Makerere University community.

Our Values

  • Accessibility
  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • User-centeredness
